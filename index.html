How this attempt works

  • 1
    110 questions, randomly drawn from the bank each attempt — no two sittings are the same.
  • 2
    Only 90 count. Twenty are unscored pretest items, exactly as on the real exam. You won't know which, so answer them all.
  • 3
    Instant feedback. Pick an answer and it locks — you immediately see correct/incorrect plus the manual-based explanation.
  • 4
    Flag and review. Mark anything to revisit, jump around with the question grid, and track your pace against the 150-minute clock.
  • 5
    Progress auto-saves on this device. Close the tab and your timed attempt resumes where you left off.
